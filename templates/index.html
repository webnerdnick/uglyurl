UglyURL

The opposite of a URL shortener

Tired of short, clean URLs? Make them long, ugly, and filled with fake tracking parameters!

Clean Hideous

How it works

Before:
https://example.com
After:
https://urlyurl.me/track/redirect/?utm_source=google_ads&utm_campaign=spring_sale_abc123&fbclid=IwAR2XxJ8kQpLmNo&ref=aHR0cHM6Ly9leGFtcGxlLmNvbQ...

Your URL is encoded and hidden among fake tracking parameters. When someone clicks the ugly URL, they'll be redirected to your original URL.

Features

  • Adds fake UTM tracking parameters
  • Generates fake Facebook, Google, and Microsoft click IDs
  • Creates meaningless path segments
  • Your URLs are never stored
